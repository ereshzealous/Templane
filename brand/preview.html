Templane — icon directions

Five starting points. Warm, humanist palette — terracotta, ochre, forest, cream, charcoal. No blue or purple.

01 · Layered Planes

Three stacked angled rectangles — evokes "template" + "plane". Top plane carries the conformance check.

02 · Paper + Seal

A document with a wax seal stamped into it. Reads as "contract" and "conformance" — closest to the tagline.

03 · Folded Template

Two stacked sheets, top one has a folded corner and a green check. "Validated template" — softest, most human feel.

04 · T Monogram (grid)

A "T" built out of typed schema cells. Structured but warm; holds up at favicon size the best.

05 · { ✓ } Bracket

Hand-drawn braces with a green check inside — typed template contract, most "human/handwritten" feel.
